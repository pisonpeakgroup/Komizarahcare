Cornrows

A braided hairstyle known as cornrows includes plaiting the hair in rows or other designs very near to the scalp. They can be styled in a variety of ways, such as all-back, ponytail, lemonade, goddess, or feed-in braids, and they can be done with or without extensions. With the right upkeep and care, cornrows are a protective hairstyle that can endure for several weeks. They work with all textures and hair types.

Braid

Another type of braided hairstyle is the braid, which is created by sectioning the hair and twisting it in various designs. They come in a range of sizes, lengths, and colours, and can be done with or without extensions. Braids are another protective hairstyle that, with proper care and upkeep, can endure for several weeks or months. They work with all textures and hair types.

Weaves

Weaves are a type of hair extension that include sewing, glueing, or clipping synthetic or human hair to the natural hair. They can be used to enhance the natural hair's length, volume, colour, or texture. There are numerous ways to style weaves, including tracks, half-head, full-head, leave-out, invisibility, closure, frontal, and 360-degree frontal styles. To keep weaves appearing clean and wholesome, regular washing, conditioning, and style are necessary.

Natural Hair Special Touch

A service known as "natural hair special touch" involves treating and styling natural hair without the use of chemicals or heat. The natural hair can be washed, conditiond, trimmed, detangled, moisturised, twisted, curled, or set. Enhancing the texture, lustre, and elasticity of natural hair with unique touch can benefit the hair's health and beauty.

Hair caring services

The provision of advise and direction on how to care for the hair and scalp constitutes hair caring services. They might involve suggesting specific hair type and condition-appropriate products, treatments, routines, or recommendations. Services for caring for your hair can help prevent or treat issues with your hair including dryness, breakage, split ends, dandruff, or thinning.
