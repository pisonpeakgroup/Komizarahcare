Komi Zarah Care offers these services and products and can also provide product recommendation and delivery to clients. This means we can suggest products that are compatible with your hair type, style, and needs. The products can include shampoo, conditioner, oil, cream, gel, spray, or accessories. We can also arrange for the products to be delivered to your address within a reasonable time frame. This way, you can enjoy the convenience and quality of the salon’s services and products at home.

A brief description of the services are Cornrows: A type of braided hairstyle that involves plaiting the hair close to the scalp in rows or patterns. Braids are another type of braided hairstyle that involves dividing the hair into sections and twisting them together in different patterns. Weaves are a type of hair extension that involves attaching artificial or human hair to the natural hair by sewing, gluing, or clipping. Natural hair special touch is a service that involves treating and styling the natural hair without using any chemicals or heat. Hair caring services are services that involve providing advice and guidance on how to care for the hair and scalp.
